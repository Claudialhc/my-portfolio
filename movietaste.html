MovieTaste

MovieTaste is an application created by three coding students, Claudia, Roman, and Brandon from San Diego, California. Our goal is to turn a mundane task, such as looking for a movie, to an entertaining task. We have cured boredom by enabling you to search for a new movie to watch by giving you information about the movie while at the same time displaying Giphy’s related to your title.
